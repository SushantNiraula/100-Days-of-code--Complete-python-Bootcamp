    4. Sushant Pizza reciepe

    Ingridents

    For Dough:

    • 2 cups of flour
    • 1/2 cup of water
    • 1/4 cup of oil
    • 1 tsp of salt
    • 1 tsp of sugar

    For Toppings:

    • 1 cup of cheese
    • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
    • 1/4 cup of bell peppers
    • 1/4 cup of onions
    • 1/4 cup of mushrooms

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
    2. In a bowl, mix flour, water, oil, salt, and sugar to make the dough.
    3. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking tray.
    4. Spread tomato sauce on the dough.
    5. Add cheese and toppings of your choice.
    6. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
